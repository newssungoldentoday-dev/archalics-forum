1. Free for Individuals

Learn, build, and share without restriction. Private use and modification is always free. No obligations if you are not a Threshold Entity.

2. Fair for Growth

Startups under 100 employees & $5M revenue use it free. Once you scale past Threshold, you give back via sponsor, commercial license, or Delayed Open.

3. Protected for Maintainers

No Threshold Entity may offer Archa-licensed work as a standalone hosted substitute without agreement. Big Tech protection built-in (Sec 3.3).
